Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 7, 2025

1. Information We Collect

SocialHub collects the following information:

  • Instagram account information (username, profile data)
  • Content you create for posting (text, images)
  • Analytics data from connected social media accounts
  • Email address and account credentials

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage and schedule your social media posts
  • Display analytics and insights
  • Authenticate your account
  • Improve our services

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest and in transit. Access tokens are stored securely and refreshed automatically.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

5. Instagram Data Use

We access Instagram data through official APIs only for:

  • Displaying your posts and media
  • Showing engagement metrics
  • Scheduling and publishing content (with proper permissions)

6. Data Deletion

You can disconnect your Instagram account at any time from the dashboard. To request complete data deletion, contact us at privacy@socialhub.app

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure token storage
  • Regular security updates

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@socialhub.app
